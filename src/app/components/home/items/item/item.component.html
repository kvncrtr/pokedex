Odd Incense

nº11

Category: Hold Item

Cost: 9,600

Effect

The Odd Incense (Japanese: あやしいおこう Mysterious Incense) is a type of held item introduced in Generation IV. It is an incense that allows Mr. Mime and Mr. Rime to produce Mime Jr. Eggs. In battle, its effect is identical to the Twisted Spoon.
